About APYER

APYER is the studio of Christian Riley. The work explores self-reflection, identity, and social tension through oil, mixed media, and digital forms.

Artist Statement

I look for the point where discomfort becomes discovery. Layers build and erode to reveal a quieter ground—an invitation for the viewer’s own catharsis.
